This is an interactive graph of the connections in the neocortical microcircuit. Boxes left and right are pre- and post-synaptic circuit layers or morphological types.

Navigation

Click on left (pre-synaptic) or right (post-synaptic) boxes to focus on a circuit layer or morphological type (m-type). Click on a connection to focus on a pathway (m-type to m-type). To defocus pre- or post-synaptic selections, click on the bar which appears to the far left or right of the sankey diagram, respectively.

Connection thickness

pathways: number of viable pathways

connections: total number of connections

synapses: total number of synapses
